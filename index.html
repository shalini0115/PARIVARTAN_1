ABOUT US

At Parivartan, we are committed to tackling Delhi's water challenges by integrating technology, community engagement, and sustainable practices. With a population exceeding 20 million, Delhi faces critical issues like water scarcity, groundwater depletion, and pollution. "Parivartan" is designed to address these challenges head-on with innovative solutions. Leveraging IoT sensors, advanced data analytics, and AI-driven insights, our platform monitors water quality, flow, and usage patterns in real-time. We focus on reducing wastage, optimizing water distribution, and enabling smarter resource management. From protecting the Yamuna River to implementing efficient urban water designs, Parvaah works to safeguard Delhi's water future. With an emphasis on public awareness, community involvement, and cutting-edge technology, Parivartan envisions a future where every drop of water is valued and managed sustainably. Join us in making every drop count and building a water-secure Delhi!
